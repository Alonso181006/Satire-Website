AI’s Involvement with the Job Market

Since Chat GPT’s release, everyone in the world is asking themselves if their job is safe. Is AI going to take over the world? Satire Spectrum journalists gathered AI software engineers to gather their thoughts on this pressing question.

“I don’t think AI is going to take over the world, it's just going to run everything around us,” Senior Engineer Gonzales stated in his interview. “Sure AI will manage all the stuff in our homes, be driving our cars and be making every decision for us, but it's not going to overthrow our government or anything! Well, it’ll be a few years (before that) at least.”

A different angle was volunteered by a confident intern. “AI can’t take our job, we make the damn thing! Marketers and stuff though, you’re pretty screwed,” he stated with a chuckle. He went on to provide “marketers and stuff” alternative job routes, such as crocheting and artisanal cheese making.

Contrasting the interns statement, an anonymous higher up gave us the inside scoop. “We’ve already replaced about half of our workers with our own AI. Simple, tedious jobs, like rewriting code, texting my kids and stuff like that. Fortunately, we, the company, are beating the punch before the government puts in any regulations about diversity(human) hires,” she offered with pride. “AI doesn’t complain about wages or office pizza parties, so hopefully we’ll extend our AI hires before regulations are in place.”

Many opposing views are seen in just one company, it’s safe to say that the future is uncertain, and filled with AI. Should we welcome our AI superiors and invest in cheesecloths? Only time will tell.
