US Presidential Campaign

Coming up soon this year is the 2024 presidential campaign that puts reality TV star Donald Trump against the grandpa himself: Joe Biden. Surprisingly, Trump has been able to surge ahead in popularity despite some of his concerning actions. The race is drawing attention from all across the United States and the world.

Donald Trump has turned his campaign into a reality TV show called "Trump: The White House 2.0”. His rallies, complete with pyromaniacs and opioid overdoses, resemble rock concerts more than political gatherings.

Trump’s most recent slogan, “Make America Entertaining Again,” seems to resonate with voters that are eager to embrace a country founded on drama and instability. Clearly the average citizen isn’t what it once was.

Rumors also suggest that as part of Trump’s TV show, he will build a wall around Washington D.C. The process would be live-streamed by popular streamers IShowSpeed and Adin Ross, and would be commentated by David Attenborough. Quite the show!

Joe Biden, on the other hand, is confident in his “Tired Town Hall” meetings. His town halls involve bean bag chairs and melatonin. He describes his policies in painstaking detail until he inevitably dozes off mid sentence. His campaign has also started handing out free coffee and pillows, turning his events into nap sessions for attendees. The slogan “Keep Calm and Nap On” has yet to gain traction, which he’s not a fan of.

As the election nears, Americans are faced with a choice between the reality TV chaos of Trump and the sleepy comfort of Biden. In true 2024 fashion, the most thrilling aspect of this race is its sheer absurdity.
