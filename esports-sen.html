Senntinels on Vacation

The professional Valorant team “Sentinels” decided the pro scene wasn’t for them after they failed to qualify for the 2024 Masters Shanghai event. This defeat was especially pitiful considering they had just won the first of the three biggest tournaments of the year. In order to cope with their failures, instead of practicing for Champions this summer, the Sentinels decided to have chicken fights at a pool in Cancun, Mexico.

The video shows Sentinels players “Zellsis” and “Zekken” on the shoulders of team staff, duking it out in a beautiful resort pool. While their choice of activities does look fun, their behavior is a complete waste of company money and time. One may argue that a vacation is needed, but that vacation should take place after this year’s championship.

It is important to note that since February of this year, Sentinels IGL (in-game leader) “Zellsis”, along with the rest of the team, has been advertising an in-game Valorant purchase that financially supports staff and players in Sentinels known as the “SEN bundle”. So, money from fans across the world that was meant to fund Sentinels ended up sponsoring their vacation instead. It is disheartening to see such a successful team not only perform so miserably at such a high level but also take advantage of their supportive fans.

To make matters worse, the team doesn’t even seem to understand the silliness of their behavior. “Boys will be boys” says the person behind the camera. This is but a feeble excuse for the team’s unprofessional actions.

The Sentinels can’t keep getting away with this. With support from all of you across the world, we can stop their reign of ridiculousness.

Please DON’T buy the SEN bundle.
